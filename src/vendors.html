Meet Our Vendors

Lakota Bows & Arrows

Oregon based family of Lakota artists Ann Shabi, Marshall Burnette, and Madison Burnette of Lakota Bows & Arrows/ Tuswecha Zi Creations have been honing their skills at crafting pre-colonial art (flint-knapping, sinew back bows and brain tanning), beading and metalsmithing as a family since 1999. As a family of artists, they use inspiration from their tribes to create heirloom quality art/accessories that celebrate their roots and history.

Seeds of Joy

Oregon based artist Alexis Rood of Seeds of Joy has a deep passion for her art, working with hand-made beaded jewelry, as well as painted bones and skulls. All of which are made with love, care and time. Alexis is extremely passionate for her art, and is happy to share it with everyone.

Becky's Rooted Life

Oregon based artist Becky White of Becky's Rooted Life is Cherokee, Cheyenne and Arapaho, on her father's side, and has been making art her entire life and creating beaded jewelry and accessories for over 30 years. Taught by elders in her community in middleschool Becky has found her passion and is more than happy to work with you to create custom pieces for any occasion.

Aunties Creationz

Oregon based artist Megan von Pelt, of Aunties Creationz, has deep ties to her plateau people and culture. Enrolled Umatilla and tracing descent from the Cayuse and Nez Perce, she celebrates her ancestry and living culture in her work ranging from dentalium earrings, to ribbon skirts, and beadwork.
