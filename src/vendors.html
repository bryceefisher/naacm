Lakota Bows & Arrows

Oregon based family of artist Ann Shabi, Marshall Burnette, and Madison Burnette of Lakota Bows & Arrows/ Tuswecha Zi Creations have been honing their skills of pre-colonial art (flint-knapping, sinew back bows and brain tanning), beading and metalsmithing as a family since 1999. As a family of artist, we use inspiration from our tribes to create heirloom quality art/accessories.
