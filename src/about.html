Eugene Native American Arts and Crafts Market

The First Sunday Eugene Native American Arts & Crafts Market (NAACM) is a gathering of Native American and Indigenous artists and crafters from a diverse representation of Tribal Nations. The NAACM is held on the first Sunday of the month at the Farmers Market Pavilion, July thru December 2023, 10am-4pm.

History

The Eugene NAACM is derived from the annual Winter NAACM which began in 2013 and held annually at the Amazon Community Center. It was composed of a group of Native community friends and family who lived in Eugene and Springfield to bring local Native American community artists and crafters together to sell their creations to our local Eugene and Springfield communities during the winter holiday season.
