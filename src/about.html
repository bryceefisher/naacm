Eugene Native American Arts and Crafts Market

The First Sunday Eugene Native American Arts & Crafts Market (NAACM) is a gathering of Native American and Indigenous artists and crafters from a diverse representation of Tribal Nations. The NAACM is held on the first Sunday of the month at the Farmers Market Pavilion, July thru December 2023, 10am-4pm.
