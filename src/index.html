About

Come experience the vibrant artistry of Native American and Indigenous talents from a mosaic of Tribal Nations! Born from the legacy of the 2013 Eugene Winter arts market, we now light up the Farmer's Market Pavilion in Eugene every first Sunday of the month. From our roots in Eugene and Springfield, we've grown in vision and reach. Passionate local Native volunteers drive this journey, dedicated to amplifying authentic Native art and crafting the perfect stage for our artisans.
